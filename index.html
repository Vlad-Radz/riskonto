Description

RiskOnto provides a concise ontology for representing concepts relevant for risks assessment.

IRI https://w3id.org/risky/riskonto#hasProbability

Indicates the probability of an event in terms of statistical value i.e. what is the value between 0.0 (never) and 1.0 (certain) for occurence of an event. The value may be quantified to produce qualitative labels similar to likelihood e.g. rarely (0.1) or likely (0.5).
